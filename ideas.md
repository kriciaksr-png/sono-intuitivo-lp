# Mentoria Sono Intuitivo — Design Brainstorm

## Approach 1: Soft Luxury Minimalism
**Design Movement**: Contemporary Luxury Wellness (inspired by Oura Ring, Calm, Apple Health)

**Core Principles**:
- Extreme whitespace and breathing room between elements
- Subtle gradients and soft shadows creating depth without visual noise
- Typography-driven hierarchy with generous line-height
- Restraint in color—use the palette sparingly, let white dominate

**Color Philosophy**:
The palette is present but whispered. Warm ivory as the dominant base, with dusty pink and muted lavender appearing only in CTAs and key moments. This creates a sense of luxury through absence rather than saturation. The restraint signals premium positioning.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Asymmetric vertical flow with alternating left-right text/image placement. Hero is full-bleed with subtle overlay. Sections have generous top/bottom padding (80-120px). Text blocks are narrow (max 600px) for readability. Cards float with minimal shadows.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Soft gradient overlays on imagery (warm ivory to transparent)
2. Floating glassmorphic cards with backdrop blur
3. Thin accent lines (1-2px) in dusty pink separating sections

**Interaction Philosophy**:
Interactions are subtle and intentional. Hover states reveal depth through slight scale and shadow increase. Scroll animations fade elements in gently. CTAs have a soft glow that appears on hover, never aggressive.

**Animation**:
- Fade-in on scroll with 300ms ease-out
- Soft parallax at 0.3 speed (not dramatic)
- CTA pulse: opacity 0.8→1 over 2s infinite, extremely subtle
- Card hover: scale 1.02 + shadow increase, 200ms ease-out
- Floating elements: 3-4px vertical movement over 3s ease-in-out

**Typography System**:
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, elegant) for headlines—weights 600, 700
- Body: Inter (sans-serif) for copy—weights 400, 500
- Hierarchy: 48px/3rem for main headlines, 28px/1.75rem for subheadings, 16px/1rem for body
- Line-height: 1.6 for body, 1.2 for headlines
- Letter-spacing: +0.5px on headlines for luxury feel

## Approach 2: Modern Editorial Boldness
**Design Movement**: Contemporary Editorial Design (inspired by luxury magazine layouts, Kinfolk aesthetic)

**Core Principles**:
- Bold, oversized typography that commands attention
- Strategic use of negative space combined with dense text blocks
- Asymmetric grid layout with intentional misalignment
- High contrast between sections through color blocking

**Color Philosophy**:
Use the palette more boldly. Orchid purple and sage green appear as full-section backgrounds, creating visual rhythm. Dusty pink is used for accent lines and highlights. This approach treats color as structural, not decorative—each color has a purpose in the layout hierarchy.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Diagonal cuts and angled transitions between sections. Hero has a bold typography overlay on the image. Sections alternate between full-width color blocks and white space. Text wraps around images in editorial style. Cards are arranged in asymmetric grids (not uniform).

**Signature Elements**:
1. Bold diagonal SVG dividers between sections
2. Large pull quotes in dusty pink
3. Oversized numbers or icons as visual anchors

**Interaction Philosophy**:
Interactions are more pronounced. Hover states reveal additional information. Scroll reveals animations are faster and more noticeable. CTAs have a distinct hover state with color shift.

**Animation**:
- Staggered entrance animations for card groups (80ms between items)
- Slide-in from edges on scroll (400ms ease-out)
- CTA: color shift on hover + slight scale (1.05), 150ms ease-out
- Diagonal wipe transitions between sections
- Floating elements with more pronounced movement (6-8px)

**Typography System**:
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) for headlines—weights 700, 800
- Accent: Poppins (sans-serif, modern) for subheadings—weights 600
- Body: Inter (sans-serif)—weights 400, 500
- Hierarchy: 56px/3.5rem for main headlines, 32px/2rem for subheadings, 16px/1rem for body
- Line-height: 1.5 for body, 1.1 for headlines
- Letter-spacing: +1px on headlines for boldness

## Approach 3: Wellness Tech Sophistication
**Design Movement**: Feminine Tech Design (inspired by Notion, Figma, premium SaaS)

**Core Principles**:
- Clean, geometric design with rounded corners and soft edges
- Data-driven visual hierarchy using size and weight
- Glassmorphism and layered transparency creating depth
- Consistent micro-interactions that feel responsive and alive

**Color Philosophy**:
The palette is used in a sophisticated gradient system. Backgrounds layer multiple colors with opacity to create depth. Orchid purple and sage green blend together in subtle gradients. This approach uses color as a dimension, not just surface decoration.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Grid-based but with intentional breaks. Cards are the primary unit—floating, stacked, and layered. Sections have consistent padding but vary in content arrangement. Floating elements create a sense of three-dimensionality. Everything feels modern and tech-forward.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Layered glassmorphic cards with varied opacity
2. Gradient accents (pink to lavender to green)
3. Animated progress indicators and micro-visualizations

**Interaction Philosophy**:
Interactions are fluid and responsive. Every hover state reveals additional depth. Scroll animations are smooth and continuous. CTAs feel interactive and responsive to user intent.

**Animation**:
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations (500ms ease-in-out)
- Card entrance: scale from 0.95 + opacity 0 to 1 + opacity 1
- Hover: shadow increase + slight lift (translateY -2px), 200ms ease-out
- CTA: gradient animation on hover, 300ms ease-out
- Floating elements: gentle rotation (2-3°) combined with vertical movement

**Typography System**:
- Display: Sora (modern sans-serif) for headlines—weights 600, 700
- Body: Inter (sans-serif)—weights 400, 500
- Accent: Poppins for emphasis—weights 600
- Hierarchy: 52px/3.25rem for main headlines, 30px/1.875rem for subheadings, 16px/1rem for body
- Line-height: 1.6 for body, 1.15 for headlines
- Letter-spacing: +0.3px on headlines

---

## Selected Approach: Soft Luxury Minimalism

**Why this approach?** This design philosophy aligns perfectly with the "Sono Gentil" positioning. The restraint and whitespace communicate premium positioning without aggression. The subtle animations feel calming rather than stimulating—appropriate for a sleep mentorship brand. The asymmetric layout avoids the generic template feel. This approach will convert through trust and sophistication, not through visual noise.

**Implementation commitment**: All design decisions will reinforce this philosophy. Whitespace is sacred. Colors appear strategically. Typography is elegant and readable. Animations are subtle and purposeful. The result will feel like a luxury wellness brand, not a Brazilian infoproduct.
